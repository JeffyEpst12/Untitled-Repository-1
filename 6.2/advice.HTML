Tips for Buying and Restoring a Classic Car



When buying a classic car, here are a few important things to look for:

  • Set a Budget: Factor in purchase price, restoration costs, and maintenance.
  • Choose the Right Model: Research popular cars with good parts availability and resale value.
  • Inspect Body & Frame: Check for rust, corrosion, and structural damage.
  • Check Engine & Mechanics: Ensure the engine runs smoothly, with no leaks or issues.
