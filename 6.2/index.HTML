About Jack Jetson

Jack Jetson is passionate about restoring classic Minis. His work brings new life to vintage cars, and he is dedicated to providing quality restoration services. Jack is also involved in organizing classic car rallies in the area. He uses a large garage as his workshop, with several restoration projects in progress.
